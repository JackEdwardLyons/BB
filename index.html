About

A global community of mountain-lovers at the click of a button.

BETA Buddy is a revolutionary new platform connecting climbers, campers, hikers, rangers, dirtbags and all round adventure travellers.

Users can share stories, create real-time mountain and crag updates as well as ask questions on all things travel, climbing and adventure.



Discover the mountains like a local.

With BETA Buddy you can map out your perfect mountain adventure with your new buddies from around the world. Plus, BETA Buddy can be used when travelling, hiking, climbing, camping...
You name it.

This means you can travel the world and explore the outdoors with peace of mind knowing that you can easily keep up to date 24 hours a day.

Share crag chat, travel tales & legendary BETA.

BETA Buddy is a robust platform where users can establish a profile and easily connect with other like minded adventure addicts!

Now you can spread the news about your latest send, profile your newest project, add pictures, videos & maps and documents all in the one place. Welcome to the future of real time crag chat.

Why we exist

BETA Buddy was created with one goal in mind: Find a solution that allows mountain-lovers around the world to easily connect, share stories, relay important information & organise awesome adventures.

BETA Buddy was built out of a simple desire to find relevant, real time information from true locals who know their mountains best.

Because the world is full of amazing mountains just waiting to be explored.
